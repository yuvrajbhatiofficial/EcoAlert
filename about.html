About Us

Welcome to EcoAlert, a revolutionary application founded in January 2024 by Yuvraj Bhati, dedicated to empowering individuals to take small yet impactful steps towards fostering a positive change in our environment. Yuvraj Bhati, the visionary founder, has been passionate about environmental sustainability and recognized the need for a platform that enables users to actively contribute to the betterment of the world around us. Guided by his vision, EcoAlert has become a powerful tool for users to voice their concerns and engage in community-driven initiatives addressing various environmental challenges, such as trees, plants, street dogs, waste management, and garbage accumulation on side
At the helm of EcoAlert is our one and only CEO, Mohammed Kaif, who brings a wealth of leadership experience and a commitment to driving positive change. With Mohammed Kaif's guidance, the app has evolved into a user-friendly platform that allows anyone with a smartphone to easily access the power to express their concerns and participate in collective efforts to tackle environmental issues. Together, Yuvraj Bhati and Mohammed Kaif lead the charge in inspiring a sense of responsibility and awareness, fostering a community that actively contributes to the preservation and enhancement of our shared environment. Join us on this journey towards a greener and healthier planet, where, under their leadership, we believe we can make a lasting impact, one small step at a time.
